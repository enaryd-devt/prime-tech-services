Sale Price Control

Enhance your Odoo 18 experience with our Sale Price Control.

This module adds a Minimum Sale Price Control feature for products in Odoo. It ensures that sales orders cannot be confirmed if any product is sold below its defined minimum price. This feature is especially useful for companies that want to protect their profit margins and maintain consistent pricing policies across their sales operations. By enforcing minimum price rules, it helps prevent sales representatives from offering excessive discounts or selling products at a loss. Access to this functionality is limited to users in the “Sale Price Control” group, ensuring that only authorized users can use and manage this feature.

Automated Validation

Automatically checks unit prices during order confirmation, reducing manual errors.

Manual Configuration Option

The feature works only when the sale order cost control setting is manually enabled in the system configuration. This allows administrators to decide when the minimum sale price validation should be active, providing flexibility in managing pricing rules.
